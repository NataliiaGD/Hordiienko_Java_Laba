Some interesting facts about airports:

1. Busiest Airport in the World: Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport in Atlanta, Georgia,
USA, is the world's busiest airport in terms of passenger numbers and overall traffic.
2. Highest Airport in the World: Daocheng Yading Airport in Tibet, China, situated at an elevation of
approximately 4,411 meters, is the highest airport globally.
3. Speedy Departures and Arrivals: Charles de Gaulle Airport in Paris has installed moving walkways to
help passengers quickly navigate through the airport.
4. Smallest Airport in the World: Juancho E. Yrausquin Airport on the island of Saba in the Caribbean Sea
is considered the world's smallest commercial airport, with a runway length of only 400 meters.
5. Passport-Free Airport: Amsterdam's Schiphol Airport is recognized as one of the airports without passport
control for flights within the European Union. Passengers can go through security without using a passport.
